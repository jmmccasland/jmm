RockSpot

RockSpot is an application that connects musicians with inspirational environments in which to create. My two partners, Brannon Crumpton and Jeff Brinkley, and I developed this over the course of three weeks as our final project at The Iron Yard. The project is not currently deployed while we are working on an actual launch, but for now you can view it by downloading the repository on GitHub and running a local server on your computer. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Forty Ten Barber Studio

This fantastic group of barbers contacted me about building a static site to display their prices and services and funnel their clients to an external booking software. Although the timeline was tight, I still had a blast working with them! To add a small amount of functionality and create a better UI, I added a sticky button that follows along the bottom of the viewport until it hits the footer, ensuring the call to action is always a thumb tap away from the user.
